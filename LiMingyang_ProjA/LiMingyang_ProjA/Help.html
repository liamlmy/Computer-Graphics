Help - User Instruction

Author: Mingyang Li

Happy Chinese Rooster Year!

This is a 3D graphic of a rooster and a sun.

As you can see in the website, the graphic is automatically moving and spinning to some extent.

Besides, the color of the graphic is changing automatically, and the shape of the sun is also changing smoothly overtime.

There are several buttons under the graphic which have different functions.

You can you use your mouse to drag in the region of graphic to change the two objects' angle

Also you can press the key Q, W, E, R, T to implement different functions.

Button LESS CLOCKWISE

To click on this button, you can speed down the spinning in the direction of clockwise or speed up the spinning in the direction of unclockwise.

Button MORE CLOCKWISE

To click on this button, you can speed up the spinning in the direction of clockwise or speed down the spinning in the direction of unclockwise.

Button PAUSE/RESTART

To click on this button, you can pause the graphic to not be a static figure. Or you can restart the graphic to move and spin.

Button SPEEDUP

To click on this button, you can speed up the spinning.

Button SPEEDDOWN

To click on this button, you can speed down the spinning.

Button RESET

To click on this button, you can reset the movement of the graphic to the original state.

Button clear

To click on this button, you can clear all operations.

Keyboard Q

To press this key, you can speed down the spinning in the direction of clockwise or speed up the spinning in the direction of unclockwise.

Keyboard W

To press this key, you can speed up the spinning in the direction of clockwise or speed down the spinning in the direction of unclockwise.

Keyboard E

To press this key, you can pause the graphic to not be a static figure. Or you can restart the graphic to move and spin.

Keyboard R

To press this key, you can speed up the spinning.

Keyboard T

To press this key, you can speed down the spinning.

Mouse click and drag

If you click in the right part of the graphic, the rooster will move right in one step.
If you click in the left part of the graphic, the rooster will move left in one step.
If you click in the top part of the graphic, the rooster will move up in one step.
If you click in the down part of the graphic, the rooster will move down in one step.
If you click in the lower right of the graphic, the rooster will move both down and right in one step.
If you click in the upper right of the graphic, the rooster will move both up and right in one step.
If you click in the lower left of the graphic, the rooster will move both down and left in one step.
If you click in the upper left of the graphic, the rooster will move both up and left in one step.
If you drag you mouse, the graphic, the rooster and the sun will change it view of angle in according with your dragging extent.

Under the buttons, there is one paragraph to show the current angle of the two objects spinning. And there is another paragraph to show that the Mouse drag amount.
